Persian

 Persian cats are a breed of cat characterized by their long fur and round small face. Throughout breeding many people have started to create persiand with even flatter faces, which can lead to significant health problems.

Scottish Fold

 Originating in the United Kingdom, Scottish Folds are characterized by their unique ear shape, often being described as an owl-like appearance. Along with the Persian cat, it is one of the most expensive cat breeds in the world.

Siamese

 Siamese cats have a special quality, every Siamese cat share the same colors, color pattern, and eye color. Some are fluffy some are short-hared, but it is characterized for its pale coat with large black spots on its face, legs, and ears. They also all have blue eyes.
